About Us

Music Education for All is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Our Mission

To provide children and adults in our community, regardless of background or income, the opportunity to learn, grow, and thrive through music education. We aim to empower creativity, build confidence, and inspire lifelong engagement with the musical arts.

Key Initiatives

These initiatives directly support the mission to provide inclusive, high-quality music education and empower lifelong engagement:

Scholarships for Underserved Students

Provide need-based tuition assistance to ensure no student is turned away due to financial hardship.

Summer Music Camps at the YMCA or Public Libraries

Run week-long camps for kids and teens during summer breaks, combining music instruction, collaboration, and fun community activities.

Student Performance Concerts

Host regular concerts featuring student musicians to showcase their progress, build confidence, and engage the community.

Instrument Lending Library

Establish a library of instruments that students can borrow, reducing cost barriers for families.

Music Outreach to Senior Homes

Coordinate performances at local senior centers to bring joy, foster intergenerational connection, and give students real-world experience.
