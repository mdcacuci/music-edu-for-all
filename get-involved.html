Get Involved

Because Every Heart Deserves the Opportunity to Experience the Magic of Music.

At Music Education for All, we believe everyone can make a difference through music. There are many ways to support our mission to provide children and adults in our community with access to music education, build confidence, and inspire lifelong engagement with the arts.

Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteers are the heart of our programs. Your time and talents help us reach more students and create meaningful experiences. Opportunities include:

  • Music Mentoring – Teach or mentor students in piano, music theory, or performance.
  • Event Support – Assist with concerts, recitals, fundraising events, and summer camps.
  • Community Outreach – Help coordinate and teach music programs for schools, senior centers, and community spaces.

Whether you can give a few hours a month or more regularly, your involvement makes a real impact.

Financial Support

Your financial contributions ensure that every student, regardless of background or income, has the opportunity to learn and grow through music. All donations are tax-deductible. Ways to support include:

  • General Donations – Help fund programs, scholarships, and instrument access.
  • Scholarships & Tuition Assistance – Directly support students in need so they can participate in lessons and camps.
  • Program Sponsorship – Sponsor concerts, masterclasses, or summer music camps.
  • Matching Gifts – Many employers match donations, doubling the impact of your gift.
  • Instrument Donations – Donate new or gently used instruments to provide others with the tools they need to succeed.

Every gift, big or small, brings music to life for our students and strengthens our community.
