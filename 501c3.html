What is a 501(c)(3)?


A 501(c)(3) is a federally recognized nonprofit organization created to serve the public good. As a 501(c)(3) music education nonprofit, our mission is purely charitable: expanding access to high-quality music education for all.

  • All donations are tax-deductible for donors.
  • Funds must be used to support our mission, not private profit.
  • We are held to strict transparency and accountability standards.

Being a 501(c)(3) ensures that every contribution directly supports music education programs, resources, and opportunities for students who might not otherwise have access.
